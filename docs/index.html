Frequently Asked Questions

Is BreakMate really free to try?
Yes! BreakMate comes with a 7-day free trial from the Microsoft Store. You can experience all features with no limitations. After the trial, it's a one-time purchase of $2.99 — no subscriptions, no recurring charges.
Does BreakMate collect any data?
Absolutely not. BreakMate is 100% offline. It never connects to the internet, never creates accounts, and never collects or transmits any data. All your settings and statistics are stored locally on your device. You can read our full privacy policy for details.
What health reminders does BreakMate include?
BreakMate includes 5 science-backed reminder types: Eye Rest (20-20-20 rule), Hydration Tracking (8-glass daily goal), Movement Alerts, Posture Checks, and Stretching exercises. Each can be customized with intervals from 1 to 120 minutes.
Can I customize the reminder intervals?
Yes! Every reminder type has its own customizable interval from 1 to 120 minutes. You can also snooze any reminder when you're in the zone. Right-click the system tray icon to access Settings at any time.
What are the system requirements?
BreakMate requires Windows 10 version 1803 or later (x64 architecture). It's ultra-lightweight at approximately 4 MB and uses minimal system resources. No admin rights are required for installation.
